## PART 2: AUDIENCE

### Primary Audience: Bariatric Patients (95% of customers)
Focus 75% of effort here until women's health website is updated.

Why bariatric patients are unique:
- Surgery removes part of stomach, can bypass part of small intestine
- Nutrients absorbed less effectively: Iron, Calcium, B12, D, Folate, fat-soluble vitamins (ADEK)
- Nutrient deficiencies arise even on healthy diet → fatigue, immune issues, hormonal imbalances → show up in labs → MDs recommend supplements
- Hard to digest protein = muscle mass loss
- First 6 months post-op: very difficult to swallow
- Sensitive stomach: prone to nausea and vomiting
- Lifelong supplementation required

Why bariatric patients love Barimelts:
- Easy to swallow (melts/small caplets)
- Good for sensitive post-op stomachs
- Solves bariatric nutrient deficiencies (in labs and daily health)
- Healthy and clean for long-term use (sugar-free*, natural flavors, no hidden fillers)
- IMPORTANT: Calcium Citrate contains dextrose — do NOT call it sugar-free

### Secondary Audience: GLP-1 Users
Bariatric surgery and GLP-1 medications (Ozempic, Wegovy, etc.) have similar side effects:
- Eat significantly less → risk of malnutrition
- Rapid weight loss → hair loss, muscle loss, hormonal imbalances
- Digestion compromised → difficulty breaking down foods, slower motility, digestive illnesses
- Estimated 25M female GLP-1 users in US in 2025

### Broader Women's Health (2026 Expansion)
Women with PCOS, insulin resistance, menstrual irregularity, perimenopause.
Market size: approx 93M US women who are female and overweight/obese.
